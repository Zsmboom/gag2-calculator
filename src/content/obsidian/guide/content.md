# 🚀 Grow a Garden 2 — Quick Start Guide

**Breadcrumb:** Roblox > GAG2 > Getting Started

## Step 1: Plant Your First Crops

1. Visit the **Seed Shop** and buy your first seeds
2. Start with **Carrot** (costs only 1 Sheckle)
3. Plant in your garden plots
4. Wait for crops to grow (they grow offline too!)
5. Harvest and sell at the Market for Sheckles

## Step 2: Recommended Progression

| Phase | Sheckles | Target Crop | Seed Cost | Why |
|-------|----------|-------------|-----------|-----|
| 🟢 Early | 0–10K | Carrot → Tomato | 1 → 200 | Fast first cash |
| 🟢 Early | 10K–100K | **Bamboo** | 700 | Dense planting + weather mutations = best early game crop |
| 🟡 Mid | 100K–1M | Corn → Cactus | 2.5K → 5K | Stable income + defense |
| 🟡 Mid | 1M–10M | **Mushroom** | 15K | Highest base sell price Epic |
| 🔴 Late | 10M+ | Dragon Fruit → Venus Fly Trap | 120K → 400K | High value + thieves defense |
| 💎 Endgame | 100M+ | Dragon's Breath / Moon Bloom | 65M+ | Super rarity |

## Step 3: Basic Controls

| Action | Key |
|--------|:---:|
| Move | WASD |
| Harvest / Interact | E |
| Open Door / Shop | E |
| Backpack | B |
| Settings / Codes | ⚙️ (top-left) |

## Step 4: Early Strategy Tips

1. **Rush Bamboo (700🪙)** — Plant densely, wait for weather-triggered mutations
2. **Use Friend Boost** — Have friends join your server for extra sell %
3. **Don't over-invest in early gear** — Focus on more seeds and plots instead
4. **Watch the Seed Shop** — Restocks every 4–5 minutes
5. **Redeem codes** — `TEAMGREENBEAN` gives 3× Green Bean Seeds

## Important Notes

| ⚠️ | Description |
|:---:|-------------|
| 🚫 | GAG2 is a **completely separate game** from GAG1. No items or progress transfer. |
| 🌙 | Crops can be stolen at night. Harvest before logging off or deploy defenses. |
| 💬 | Join the Discord for weather event alerts and mutation timing. |
