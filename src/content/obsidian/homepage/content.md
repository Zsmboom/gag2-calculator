## Game Premise

Grow a Garden 2 is the sequel to the popular Roblox farming game. Key new features:

- **Day/Night Cycle** — Farm by day, steal crops from other gardens at night
- **Guild System** — Team up for weekly rewards
- **Map Pets** — Pets spawn randomly on the map with unique abilities
- **Weather System** — Affects crop growth and triggers mutations
- **Mutations** — Value multipliers on harvests (mutually exclusive in GAG2)
- **Offline Growth** — Crops continue growing while you're offline
- **Fresh Economy** — Nothing carries over from GAG1
