# ⏰ Offline Growth

**Breadcrumb:** Roblox > GAG2 > Mechanics > Offline Growth

## How It Works

GAG2's crops **continue growing while you're offline**. This means:

- No need to stay online waiting for crops to mature
- Every login brings a new harvest
- Crops can still be stolen while you're offline

## Best Practices

| Action | Why |
|:-------|:------------|
| ✅ Harvest high-value crops before logging off | Prevents theft loss |
| ✅ Deploy defensive pets before closing | Partial protection |
| ✅ Use Private Server if available | 100% offline safety |
| ❌ Leave garden unlocked at night | High theft risk |

> During Chinese night hours (Asia/Shanghai GMT+8), fewer players are active — but Roblox is global, so theft is still possible.
