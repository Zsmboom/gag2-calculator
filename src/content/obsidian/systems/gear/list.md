# ⚙️ Grow a Garden 2 — Gear & Tools

**Breadcrumb:** Roblox > GAG2 > Gear

> Purchased from the Gear Shop and Props Shop. Sprinklers are the most important equipment.

## 🌊 Sprinklers (Most Important)

| Name | Effect | Price |
|:----:|:--------|:-----:|
| **Basic Sprinkler** | Increases nearby crop weight (higher harvest value) | Low |
| **Advanced Sprinkler** | Stronger growth boost | Medium |
| **Super Sprinkler** | Maximum growth boost | High |

> Sprinklers increase crop **weight** — weight scales exponentially with value (power law: weight^exp). This is the most important investment after seeds.
