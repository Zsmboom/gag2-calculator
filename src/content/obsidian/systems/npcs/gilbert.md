# Gilbert — Grow a Garden 2 Guild Stand NPC

Gilbert is the Guild Stand NPC in Grow a Garden 2. He handles guild creation, the global guild leaderboard, and guild rewards. Guilds are a GAG2-exclusive feature not in the original Grow a Garden 1.

## What Gilbert Does

- **Create a Guild** — Costs **99 Robux**. Requires a main title, sub-label, and description.
- **View Current Guild** — Check your active guild's status and members.
- **Global Guild Leaderboard** — See the ranking of all guilds.
- **Guild Rewards** — Weekly competition rewards, including access to the Geode Egg.

## Location

Gilbert is located at the Guild Stand in the center of the map, near the Sell Stand and Props Shop.
