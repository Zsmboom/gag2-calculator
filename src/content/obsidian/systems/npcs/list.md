### Sam (Seed Shop)
The Seed Shop vendor. Sells rotating plantable seeds from Common to Legendary tiers. Check the live stock tracker on the wiki to see Sam's current inventory.

### George (Gear Shop)
The Gear Shop NPC. Sells sprinklers, watering cans, and other farming tools priced in Sheckles or Robux. Gear from George affects crop grow speed and garden defense.

### Charlotte (Props Shop)
The Props Shop NPC. Sells decorative and cosmetic props for player gardens. Located in the map center near the Sell Stand and Guild Stand.

### Steven (Sell Stand)
The Sell Stand NPC. Bring harvested crops to him to earn Sheckles. Payout depends on crop base value, weight, mutations, and active server sell multipliers.

### Gilbert (Guild Stand)
The Guild Stand NPC. Handles guild creation (costs 99 Robux), the global guild leaderboard, and guild rewards. Guilds are a GAG2-exclusive feature not in Grow a Garden 1.

### Auctioneer
The Auctioneer is a new NPC added in the June 28, 2026 update. Located at the Auction Stand, they sell 6 random premium items that restock every **20 minutes**. Items can include super sprinklers, super watering cans, mythical seeds, and super seeds.

**Pricing mechanism:** Dutch auction — each item starts at an extremely high price (potentially billions of Sheckles) and gradually decreases over time. Rarer items have fewer units in stock.

**Strategy:** Buy early if you need a rare item and can afford the premium, or wait for the price to drop and risk someone else buying it first.
