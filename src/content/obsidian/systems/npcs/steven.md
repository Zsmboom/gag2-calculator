# Steven — Grow a Garden 2 Sell Stand NPC

Steven is the Sell Stand NPC in Grow a Garden 2. Bring harvested crops to him to earn Sheckles. Payout depends on crop base value, weight, mutations, and active server sell multipliers.

## How the Sell Stand Works

Steven buys your harvested crops for Sheckles. The final payout is calculated from:

- **Crop Base Value** — The 1kg base value of the crop
- **Weight** — Higher weight = higher payout (non-linear curve)
- **Mutations** — Active mutations multiply the sell price (e.g. Gold ×10, Electric ×25)
- **Sell Multipliers** — Active server sell multipliers (can vary in real-time)

## Location

Steven is located at the Sell Stand in the center of the map, near the Props Shop and Guild Stand.
