# George — Grow a Garden 2 Gear Shop Vendor

George is the Gear Shop NPC in Grow a Garden 2. He sells sprinklers, watering cans, and other farming tools priced in Sheckles or Robux. Gear from George affects crop grow speed and garden defense.

## What George Sells

George's inventory includes:
- **Sprinklers** — Automate crop watering
- **Watering Cans** — Manual watering tools (including Super variants)
- **Farming Tools** — Various gear that affects crop grow speed and garden defense

Items are priced in either Sheckles (🪙) or Robux (💎).

## Location

George is located at the Gear Shop, a dedicated shop area in the game map.
