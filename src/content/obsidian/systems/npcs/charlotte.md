# Charlotte — Grow a Garden 2 Props Shop Vendor

Charlotte is the Props Shop NPC in Grow a Garden 2. She sells decorative and cosmetic props for player gardens. The Props Shop is in the map center near the Sell Stand and Guild Stand, making it easy to visit between harvests.

## What Charlotte Sells

Charlotte runs the Props Shop and is the only NPC that sells decorative and cosmetic items for player gardens. Specific prop items and their prices are not yet fully documented.

## Location

Charlotte is located at the Props Shop in the center of the map, near the Sell Stand and Guild Stand. The central location makes it convenient to visit after selling crops.
