# Sam — Grow a Garden 2 Seed Shop Vendor

Sam is the Seed Shop vendor in Grow a Garden 2 and usually the first NPC new players interact with. He sells rotating plantable seeds from common to legendary tiers depending on the current stock rotation.

## What Sam Sells

Sam sells plantable seeds that rotate on a stock timer. His inventory can include seeds from Common to Legendary tiers depending on the current rotation. Checking Sam's stock before a session is a standard part of planning which crops to grow.

## Location

Sam is located at the Seed Shop. The Seed Shop is where most players start — it stocks seeds that can be planted in your garden. Seed stock refreshes every ~4-5 minutes.
