# Auctioneer — Grow a Garden 2

The Auctioneer is an NPC in Grow a Garden 2 who runs a Dutch auction stand with 6 random premium items. Added on **June 28, 2026** as part of the Hypno Bloom & Auctioneer Update.

## Key Stats

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Role | Auction |
| Location | Auction Stand |
| Added | 2026-06-28 |
| Restock Cycle | Every 20 minutes |
| Items Per Cycle | 6 random premium items |

## How the Dutch Auction Works

1. When the Auction Stand restocks, each of the 6 items starts at an **extremely high price** (potentially billions of Sheckles)
2. The price **gradually decreases over time** until someone buys it
3. **Rarer items have fewer units** in stock (limited supply)
4. Once all units of an item are sold, it disappears until the next restock

## Items Available

The Auctioneer sells premium items that can include:

- Super Sprinklers
- Super Watering Cans
- Mythical Seeds
- Super Seeds

## Strategy

- **Buy early** if you need a rare item and can afford the premium price
- **Wait for a discount** if you're patient, but risk someone else buying the stock first
- Check the stand regularly — the 20-minute restock cycle means frequent opportunities
