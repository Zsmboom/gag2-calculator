# 🌙 Night Stealing

**Breadcrumb:** Roblox > GAG2 > Mechanics > Night Stealing

## Day/Night Cycle

| Phase | Duration |
|:-----:|:--------:|
| ☀️ Day | 7 min 30 sec |
| 🌅 Dusk | 30 sec |
| 🌙 Night | 2 min |
| **Total** | **10 min cycle** |

## How Stealing Works

1. At night, walk up to another player's unattended garden
2. Press **E** to loot crops
3. Stolen items cannot be teleported — you must walk back to your base

## How to Defend

| Method | Cost | Effectiveness |
|:-------|:----:|:-------------:|
| Stay inside your garden | Free | ✅ Best — garden locks at night |
| Harvest before logging off | Free | ✅ Safe |
| Private server | 💎 Paid | ✅ 100% safe |
| Defensive pets (Bee, Gnome) | Mid | 🟡 Good |
| Defense plants (Cactus, Venus Fly Trap, Dragon's Breath) | Varies | 🟡 Good |
| Defense gear (Vine Wrapper, Freeze Ray, Power Hose) | High | 🟡 Good |

# Grow a Garden 2 — 夜间偷窃指南

## 基础机制
- 10分钟昼夜循环中的2分钟夜间时段
- 夜间可以偷窃他人的花园
- 离线时作物仍在生长但无人防守

## 防御策略
1. **高价值作物睡前收获** — 避免被偷
2. **种植防御植物** — Venus Flytrap、Cactus、Poison Ivy
3. **使用防御宠物** — Bee、Gnome、Venus Dragonfly、**Bear**（Aurora Event 新增）
4. **使用防御装备** — Player Magnet 吸引敌人到防御位置

## 夜间偷窃技巧
- 优先偷 Mythic/Super 稀有度的作物
- 关注 Legendary 宠物刷新全服警报
- 多人协作提高偷窃效率
