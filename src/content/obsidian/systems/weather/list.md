## Aurora Borealis 极光事件 — 详细机制

> 来源: Beebom, Pro Game Guides (2026-06-20)

- **触发条件**: 夜间随机触发 Aurora Borealis 天气事件
- **效果**: 对部分作物施加 Aurora 突变，×1.5 价值乘数
- **新种子 — Venom Spitter**: Mythic 防御型植物，可攻击偷菜者，多次收获
- **新宠物 — Bear**: Mythic 级，主动攻击入侵花园的窃贼
- **新装备**:
  - Megaphone（扩音器）— 8K Sheckles，骚扰邻居
  - Player Magnet（玩家磁铁）— 7M Sheckles，将附近玩家拉近
  - Pet Teleporters（宠物传送器）— Robux 购买，传送至 Legendary/Mythic/Super 宠物
- **新机制 — DOUBLE OR NOTHING**: 出售作物时可选翻倍或全输

## Strategy by Weather

- **Rain / Thunderstorm** — Plant large batches and wait for Electric proc (×70)
- **Frost** — Ensure some crops are Wet beforehand to trigger Frozen
- **Blood Moon** — Time your largest harvest for this event (max ×80 multiplier)
- **Aurora Borealis** — Low multiplier (×1.5) but easy to trigger during night cycle. Useful for consistent bonus income.
