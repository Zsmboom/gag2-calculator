## 📊 Value Reference

When buying pets from the in-game shop, all pets are purchaseable directly with Sheckles. The most expensive are Super rarity (Ice Serpent 20M, Raccoon 15M). Cheapest are Common (Frog 10K, Bunny 20K).

## Spawn Mechanics

- Pets spawn randomly across the game map at fixed percentage chances
- Higher rarity = rarer but more powerful
- **Big version** pets cost double with enhanced abilities
- Legendary+ spawns broadcast with a server-wide message
- Ice Serpent and Black Dragon currently have 0% spawn rate (event/guild only)
