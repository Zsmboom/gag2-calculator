## Description

Bamboo is the **best early-to-mid game crop** in GAG2. Priced at only 700 Sheckles, it produces 800 S of base value at 1kg — an immediate profit. It can be planted very densely, making it ideal for mutation farming.

**Important:** Bamboo has a unique curve exponent of ~1.75 (vs ~2.65 for most crops), meaning its weight-value curve is flatter. This favors rapid cycling over weight stacking.

## Strategy

1. Save 700🪙 → buy Bamboo as soon as possible
2. Plant densely in tight clusters
3. Wait for weather mutations (Gold ×10 or Frozen ×40 = massive returns)
4. Single harvest → replant immediately
5. Use Bamboo as the springboard to Mushroom (15K seed)

## Growth Notes

This is the crop that the game's FAQ explicitly recommends as early-mid transition crop: "Rush Bamboo (700 sheckles) as your early scaling crop — plant in tight clusters and wait for weather mutations."
