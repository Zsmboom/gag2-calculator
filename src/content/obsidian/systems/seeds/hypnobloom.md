## Full Original Content

Hypno Bloom is a Super-rarity multi-harvest crop in Grow a Garden 2, added in Update 1.06.0 on June 27, 2026. It costs 90,000,000 Sheckles or 1,599 Robux per seed with a 0.275% shop restock chance. It produces 2 fruits per plant and sells for an average of 9,500 Sheckles at base weight — nearly 4x the value of Ghost Pepper at comparable weight. Plant grows in about 23 minutes; fruit regrows every 5.5 minutes. At 41 kg, a single Hypno Bloom sells for around 494,000 Sheckles with no mutations — roughly 3.5x more than a Ghost Pepper at similar weight (138,380 Sheckles at 37 kg). One Super Watering Can is enough to fully grow it.

### Ability

Like Moon Bloom's low-gravity launch, Hypno Bloom has a crowd-control effect: intruders approaching the plant are affected by a disruption that affects their movement. Exact range, duration, and whether it fully immobilizes or just disorients are not yet documented.

### How to Get

Buy from the Seed Shop for 90,000,000 Sheckles or 1,599 Robux. Restock chance ~0.275% — extremely rare, on par with Dragon's Breath. Requires a maxed economy.
