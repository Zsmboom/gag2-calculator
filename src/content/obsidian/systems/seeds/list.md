## 🧮 How Crop Value Is Calculated

```
Sell Price = Base_Value(1kg) × (Harvest_Weight)² × Mutation_Mult × (1 + FriendBoost%) × Quantity
```

Or more precisely (per-crop power fit):
```
Sell Price = coeff × weight^exp × Mutation × (1 + FriendBoost%) × Quantity
```
Where `exp` ranges from **2.0 to ~3.4** depending on the crop.

## 📈 Value Calculation Examples

**Example 1:** Mushroom, 3kg, Frozen (×40), 5% Friend Boost, 10 quantity

```
Base:    13,000 × (3/1)^2.0 = 13,000 × 9 = 117,000
× Mut:   ×40 → 4,680,000
× Boost: ×1.05 → 4,914,000
× Qty:   ×10 → **49,140,000 Sheckles**
```

**Example 2:** Bamboo, 1.5kg, Gold (×10), no friends, 1 quantity

```
Base:    800 × (1.5/1)^1.75 = 800 × 2.01 = 1,608
× Mut:   ×10 → 16,080
× Boost: ×1.0 → 16,080
× Qty:   ×1 → **16,080 Sheckles**
```
