## Description

Sun Bloom is a **Super** rarity crop in Grow a Garden 2, added in the Merging Update (July 13, 2026). It is one of the two parent plants used to merge into the **Secret**-rarity **Eclipse Bloom** (alongside Moon Bloom). Both parent plants are destroyed in the merge.

## How to Get

Buy from the Seed Shop.

## Strategy

- Key ingredient for Eclipse Bloom (merge Sun Bloom + Moon Bloom)
- Both parents are destroyed on merge — requires two separate purchases
