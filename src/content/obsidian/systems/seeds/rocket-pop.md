# 🚀 Rocket Pop — GAG2 Legendary Crop

Rocket Pop is a **Legendary single-harvest crop** in Grow a Garden 2, costing **27,500 Sheckles** or **149 Robux** per seed. Released on **July 4, 2026**.

## Overview

Rocket Pop is a Legendary single-harvest crop released on July 4, 2026 as part of Update 1.13.0. At 27,500 Sheckles per seed, it's one of the most affordable Legendary-tier crops — significantly cheaper than Cherry (1.2M) or Acorn (700K). The single-harvest type means each seed produces one crop and must be replanted.

## How to Get Rocket Pop

- Buy from the Seed Shop for 27,500 Sheckles or 149 Robux
- Standard seed shop rotation
