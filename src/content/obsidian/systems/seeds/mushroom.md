## Description

Mushroom has the **highest base sell value** of any crop in GAG2. Despite the 15K seed cost, its 13K base value at 1kg makes it a strong mid-game investment. Multi-harvest means it regrows after each harvest, providing recurring income.

## Strategy

- Purchase once you have 15K🪙+ saved
- Pair with **Frozen (×40)** or **Electric (×70)** mutations for extreme returns
- Deploy defensive pets/gear — high-value crops attract thieves
- Use Sprinklers to maximize weight gain
