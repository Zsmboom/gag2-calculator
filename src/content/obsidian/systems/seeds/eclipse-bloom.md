## Description

Eclipse Bloom is the first **Secret**-rarity crop in Grow a Garden 2, added on July 13, 2026 (Merging Update). It is multi-harvest and **cannot be bought** — it is obtained only by **merging Sun Bloom + Moon Bloom**. Both parent plants are destroyed in the merge.

This is a brand new rarity tier above Super, introduced exclusively for this crop.

## How to Get

1. Buy **Sun Bloom** (110M🪙) from the Seed Shop
2. Buy **Moon Bloom** (85M🪙) from the Seed Shop
3. **Merge them** — both are destroyed, Eclipse Bloom is created
4. Exact merge mechanic details are community-sourced (verify in-game)

## Strategy

- Requires 195M🪙+ in seed investment (Sun Bloom + Moon Bloom)
- Both parents are consumed — no refunds
- Extremely high-end endgame content
