## Description

Star Fruit is a **Super** rarity crop in Grow a Garden 2, added in the Merging Update (July 13, 2026). It glows at night and periodically strikes nearby players with 2 beams of starlight every 5-10 seconds, making it a dual-purpose crop: a farming plant and a passive night-time defense. It also occasionally gives adjacent plants the **Starfall** mutation.

Star Fruit has two color variants.

## How to Get

Buy from the Seed Shop. Restock chance is extremely rare for Super rarity crops.

## Strategy

- Dual-purpose: farming + passive defense at night
- Can trigger Starfall mutation on adjacent plants
- Endgame-tier investment due to 315M🪙 seed cost
