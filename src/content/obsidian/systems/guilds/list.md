# 🛡️ Guilds

**Breadcrumb:** Roblox > GAG2 > Mechanics > Guilds

## Overview

Guilds are a system in GAG2 that allow players to team up and compete for **weekly leaderboard rewards**.

## Known Features

- Form or join a guild with other players
- Create a guild costs **99 Robux**
- Compete in **weekly leaderboard** rankings
- **9 reward tiers** — higher ranking = better rewards
- **Top reward**: Ice Serpent pet (legendary rarity)

## Strategy

- Join an active guild early for the weekly rewards
- The Ice Serpent is one of the rarest obtainable pets — guild competition is the primary source
- Coordinate with guildmates for optimal farming during events
